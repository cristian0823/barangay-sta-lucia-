Barangay Sta. Lucia Management System

Welcome to the Barangay Sta. Lucia Portal! By creating an account and using our online services, you agree to follow the rules outlined below.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering, you agree to these Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree, please do not use the portal.

2. User Accounts & Eligibility

  • You must be a verified resident of Barangay Sta. Lucia.
  • You must provide true and accurate information during registration.
  • You are fully responsible for all activities under your account. Do not share your password.

3. Equipment Borrowing Rules

  • All equipment remains the official property of the Barangay.
  • You are responsible for picking up and returning the equipment on the exact dates agreed upon.
  • Liability: If equipment is lost or damaged while in your care, you will be required to pay for repairs or replacement.
  • Late returns may result in a temporary ban from borrowing.

4. Court Booking Rules

  • The court is for community and recreational use.
  • Bookings are subject to approval. Official barangay events always take priority.
  • Vandalism, fighting, or consuming alcohol on the premises is strictly prohibited.

5. Reporting Concerns

  • Use this feature strictly for legitimate barangay issues.
  • Spamming or submitting fake reports will result in immediate account suspension.

6. Data Privacy

Personal data collected is used solely to verify residency and improve services. We will not sell or share your data, in accordance with the Data Privacy Act.

7. Account Suspension

The Barangay Administrator reserves the right to suspend any account that violates these rules or abuses barangay privileges.
